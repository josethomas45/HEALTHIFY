Yoga: The Path to Wellness

What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to promote overall well-being.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and strength
  • Enhances mental clarity and reduces stress
  • Boosts cardiovascular health
  • Promotes better posture and balance
  • Aids in relaxation and sleep quality

Types of Yoga

  • Hatha Yoga - Gentle and slow-paced
  • Vinyasa Yoga - Flow-based movements
  • Ashtanga Yoga - Structured and intense
  • Yin Yoga - Deep tissue relaxation
  • Kundalini Yoga - Spiritual and meditative

Why Practice Yoga?

Yoga not only enhances physical health but also promotes inner peace, making it an essential part of a balanced lifestyle.
