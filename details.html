About Our Health Management Platform

Purpose

Healthify is a web-based platform designed to help users manage their health by securely storing and analyzing full-body health reports. The system provides AI-powered personalized diet and exercise recommendations while tracking users' progress through data visualization tools. The goal is to empower users to take proactive control of their well-being.

Product Perspective

Healthify is a self-contained, cloud-based health management platform to enhance health tracking and provide deeper insights. It does not rely on any wearable devices but instead utilizes uploaded health reports for AI-driven analysis. The platform operates independently and is designed for seamless user interaction through a web-based interface.

Product Functions

• Secure Upload & Storage: Users can upload full-body health reports for analysis.

• AI-Driven Analysis: AI algorithms generate personalized health insights.

• Visualization Tools: Charts and graphs help users track progress.

• Recommendation Engine: Personalized diet and exercise plans are created based on health metrics.

• User Authentication: Secure login and data access control.
