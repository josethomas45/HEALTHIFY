Push-Up Exercise

What is a Push-Up?

A push-up is a bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core muscles. It is a fundamental strength-training exercise used in various fitness programs.

How to Perform a Push-Up

1. Start in a high plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.

2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

3. Lower your chest toward the ground by bending your elbows.

4. Push back up to the starting position by straightening your arms.

5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
